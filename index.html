About

I'm a first year PhD student at University of Washington major in Computer Science. I'm a member of the PLSE group working on Programming Languages, my advisors are Ras Bodik and Alvin Cheung.

My main research interests now lies in Programming Languages and Software Engineering, especially applying programing synthesis techniques to automate software development, and particularly, I'm now looking into approaches to synthesize SQL queries from input output examples.

Before starting my graduate study in University of Washington, I received my Bachelor's degree in Peking University, majoring in Computer Science, where I worked on transformation language support for API adaptation problem with Zhenjiang Hu and Yingfei Xiong.
